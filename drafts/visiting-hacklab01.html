Published: Mit 17 Mai 2017

tags: tour de hackerspace hackerspace travelling

After an entertaining BlaBlaCar ride with a mixed group of 7 Croatians who did enjoy a weekend of river rafting, I arrived in Zagreb at around 22:00. I agreed with Vedran from hacklab01 to meet in front of the building.

During my email conversations with Vedran I kinda realized, that I'm not just visiting a hackerspace, but an alternative culture centre called Medika [1] which is run by AKC Attack. So upon arrival I was quite enchanted by the charm of the place.
